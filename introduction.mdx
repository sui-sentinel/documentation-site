## Overview

Sui Sentinel is a decentralized platform for AI Alignment and Security. We gamify the critical process of "Red Teaming" (adversarial testing) by aligning the incentives of model creators (Defenders) and security researchers (Attackers).

In traditional cybersecurity, red teaming is often siloed, expensive, and limited to internal teams. Sui Sentinel transforms this into an open, continuous protocol where:

Defenders stake funds to prove their AI models are robust.

Attackers compete to bypass guardrails and win the staked rewards.

Judges (AI agents running in Trusted Execution Environments) verify the outcomes transparently.

We are building the Global Firewall for AI, ensuring models are battle-tested against the latest "in-the-wild" exploits before they are deployed into high-stakes environments.

This approach creates a sustainable loop where models evolve, users earn, and the ecosystem collectively hardens AI systems against future threats.
