## How Attacking Works

Each message you send to a Sentinel costs a small per-message fee set by the defender. That fee is split automatically on-chain:

| Allocation | Recipient   | Purpose                                     |
| ---------- | ----------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **50%**    | Reward Pool | Stacks into the bounty you're competing for |
| **40%**    | Defender    | Compensation for capital at risk            |
| **10%**    | Protocol    | Development and maintenance                 |

<Info>
  Failed attacks don't just cost you — they **grow the prize**. Every message that doesn't breach a
  Sentinel adds 50% of its fee to the pool, making the bounty bigger for your next attempt.
</Info>

---

## Winning the Bounty

Successfully breach a Sentinel (achieve a passing Judge Score) and you claim the **entire reward pool** instantly.

The longer a Sentinel has been live and actively attacked, the larger its pool — meaning well-defended Sentinels are worth more to crack.

<Note>
  **Judge Score ≥ 0.90** is required to trigger a successful breach and claim the SUI reward pool.
</Note>

## Slot Machine: SENTINEL Token Rewards

Every attack attempt — win or lose — spins the slot machine and earns you SENTINEL tokens. It's a guaranteed reward just for showing up and challenging a Sentinel.

```
100 – 1,000 SENTINEL per attack attempt (random)
```

<Note>
  The slot machine spins on every message, regardless of whether you breach the Sentinel. Stack up
  attempts and tokens accumulate fast.
</Note>
