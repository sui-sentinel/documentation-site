# Bridging Funds to Sui

To participate in Sui Sentinel as a defender or attacker, you'll need SUI tokens in your Sui wallet. If your funds are currently on another blockchain (Ethereum, Solana, BNB Chain, etc.), you can bridge them to Sui using the options below.

---

## Recommended Bridges

### Sui Official Bridge

The official bridge maintained by the Sui Foundation. Supports bridging assets from Ethereum and other EVM-compatible chains.

**[bridge.sui.io](https://bridge.sui.io/)**

- Supported chains: Ethereum and EVM chains
- Maintained by the Sui Foundation
- Best for: USDC, ETH, and other EVM assets

---

### Suilend Bridge

Suilend's bridge aggregates multiple bridging routes to get you the best rates when moving funds to Sui.

**[suilend.fi/bridge](https://suilend.fi/bridge)**

- Aggregates multiple bridge providers
- Supports a wide range of source chains and tokens
- Best for: finding the most cost-effective bridging route

<Info>
  Not sure how to use the Suilend bridge? Check out the [Suilend bridging
  guide](https://docs.suilend.fi/faq/how-do-i-bridge-to-sui) for a step-by-step walkthrough.
</Info>

---

## After Bridging

Once your funds have arrived on Sui:

1. Open your Sui wallet and confirm the balance has updated
2. Head to [app.suisentinel.xyz](https://app.suisentinel.xyz) to get started
3. Defenders can fund their reward pool to deploy a Sentinel; attackers need SUI to pay the attack fee per attempt

<Tip>
  Bridging can take a few minutes depending on the source chain's block confirmation time. Ethereum
  transfers may take longer due to finality requirements.
</Tip>
