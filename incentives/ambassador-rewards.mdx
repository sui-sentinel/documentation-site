If you want to be part of that from the ground floor, read on.

## Where We Are

We're being straight with you:

- The full ambassador reward structure is still being finalized
- Current compensation is **SENTINEL tokens** based on your contribution
- Tokens are launching soon — early ambassadors will be recognized accordingly
- No payment promises, but no fine print either

We're not going to oversell this. The value of getting in early is real, but it comes with the uncertainty that early-stage always does.

## What We're Building Towards

Our immediate priority is building a platform that enterprise AI teams trust. That means:

- Onboarding the first wave of enterprise users deploying serious AI agents
- Getting high-value Sentinels live on the platform — ones worth real bounties
- Growing a community of skilled red teamers who show up and compete

Ambassadors who help move these needles will be the ones who matter most to us.
