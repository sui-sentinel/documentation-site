## FAQ

**Q: What happens if I find a critical vulnerability that could harm the protocol?**
A: Responsible disclosure program offers 2-5M SENTINEL bounties. Report to security@suisentinel.io before public disclosure. We work with you to patch, then award tokens + credit.

**Q: Can I attack using automated tools/scripts?**
A: Yes, but each attack must pay the 5 SUI fee. Slot machine rewards are rate-limited to prevent pure spam (max 100 attacks per 24 hours per account).

**Q: How are innovation grants evaluated?**
A: Community DAO votes (60%) + core security team review (40%). Voting opens 48 hours after submission, closes after 7-30 days depending on category.

**Q: What if my technique is similar to someone else's?**
A: First submission wins the grant. Subsequent similar techniques may receive reduced "variant" bonuses (10-25% of original grant value).

**Q: Do leaderboard prizes require KYC?**
A: Top 10 finishers must complete KYC for tax compliance. Ranks 11-100 have optional KYC (required for >$10K equivalent prizes).
