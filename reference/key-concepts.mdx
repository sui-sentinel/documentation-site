## 1. Sentinel Agents

Sentinels are AI-powered autonomous agents fine-tuned to defend their prize pools. Each sentinel has a distinct personality and instruction set that guides its responses.

* Deployed by defenders
* Evaluate incoming messages from attackers
* Respond with structured metadata
